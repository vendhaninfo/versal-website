Our Journey

Founded in 2024, Vendhan InfoTech was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to build a technology company where innovation, excellence, and inclusivity are not just ideals, but the very fabric of our operations.

As a proud women-led enterprise, we set out to challenge the status quo, proving that a diverse team is a strong team. From our roots in Oddanchatram, we have grown into a dynamic force, delivering cutting-edge solutions to clients across the globe.
